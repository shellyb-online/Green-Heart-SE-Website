Turning Waste Into Wealth: GreenHeart SE's for a Greener Ghana

Posted on June 5, 2025

At GreenHeart SE, we believe the future of Ghana—and Africa—depends on how we treat our land, our waste, and our communities today. That’s why we’ve made it our to turn agricultural and forestry waste into climate-positive products that regenerate soil, remove carbon from the atmosphere, and create sustainable livelihoods.

From transforming over 1 million tons of organic waste into biochar and clean energy, to restoring 2 million hectares of degraded land across Ghana and West Africa, GreenHeart SE is championing a circular bioeconomy built for resilience.

Our vision by 2035 is bold: to become Ghana’s circular bioeconomy powerhouse. That means regenerating ecosystems, empowering 2 million farmers and agribusinesses with regenerative tools, and capturing 2.5 million tons of CO₂-equivalent es each year.

And we’re not doing this alone. Through partnerships with farmers, cooperatives, governments, and investors, we’re scaling impact-driven solutions that are rooted in science and powered by innovation. Whether it’s biochar-based fertilizers, bio-stimulants, or digital monitoring systems, our technologies are designed to be accessible, affordable, and transformative.

Stay tuned as we share stories of change, profiles of farmers leading the green transition, and updates on how GreenHeart SE is shaping policy, research, and market leadership across Africa’s climate-smart agriculture space.

— Team GreenHeart SE
