ABOUT

GREEN HEART SE

GreenHeart SE is a pioneering agritech and circular economy enterprise based in Ghana. The company transforms agricultural and forestry waste into high-value, climate-resilient products that support sustainable farming, environmental restoration, and carbon removal. By leveraging advanced green technologies, GreenHeart SE contributes to soil regeneration, food security, and climate change mitigation while empowering local communities and farmers.

WHAT

WE OFFER

Our key products include biochar-based soil conditioners, feed supplements, broad-spectrum pesticide and bio stimulant with no microbial resistance, and (soon) activated carbon for industries such as mining, pharmaceuticals, and water filtration. This circular economy approach tackles waste management and soil degradation while supporting food security.
