ABOUT

OUR COMPANY

We turn plastic waste into opportunity!We collect discarded plastic bottles, giving them a second life by either supplying them to recycling companies or transforming them into durable, eco-friendly dustbins. Join us in creating a cleaner planet.

Our Products: Washed HDPE flakes, PET bales, washed PET flakes and waste bins made from PET

Mission: To create a sustainable plastic waste recovery and value chain that protects the environment and our environmental stakeholders.

Vision: To lead change in the plastic recycling industry through the lens of circularity, innovation and value creation.

Goal: a social venture for sustainability, recycling for a zero waste future.

THE FOUNDER & CEO

A young entrepreneur (18-35) from Ahenbrom-Mankessim, Central Region is addressing indiscriminate rubber waste disposal in his community. Challenges include high costs of existing waste solutions, lack of education on proper waste management, delays in waste transportation, absence of dumping sites, lack of waste bins, and no recycling companies in the area.

His solution is to establish a recycling company and provide waste bins to help the community properly manage waste, reduce contamination-related illnesses, and prevent soil pollution.

Operating in the Recycling, Industrial, and Renewable sectors, he seeks funding to develop and implement his waste management solution, improving environmental health and sustainability.
